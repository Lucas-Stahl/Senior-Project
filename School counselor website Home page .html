Meet the counselors themselves

Mr. Bard Mrs. Carlson

Chris is a school counselor at Berks Career & Technology Center West Campus.
He holds a Master of Science in PreK-12 School Counseling from Kutztown
University as well as a bachelor of Science in Criminal Justice from Kutztown
University. Chris is a National Certified Counselor, as well as a Trauma and
Crisis Specialist through the American School Counselor Association. He is an
active member of the American School Counselors Association Pennsylvania School
Counselors Association where he serves as the Executive Board Secretary Berks
Area School Counselor Association, Chi Sigma Iota Academic and Professional
Honor Society Pennsylvania Association of Career & Technical Administrators and
the Pennsylvania Association for College Admissions Counseling where he serves
on the Secondary School Counselor committee. He is passionate about assisting
students and making a difference in their lives. Working in education allows him
help students find their life’s mission while they are learning new skills to be
amazing humans. He feels that each student’s story, journey and needs are unique
and that is important when supporting their path to success.

Licensure in Professional Counseling, Commonwealth of PA – Dept of
State Bureau of Professional & Occupational Affairs. Master of Art
in Counseling Psychology, Immaculata University. Educational
Specialist II – Pennsylvania Certification in Elementary & Secondary
School Counseling PK-12. National Certification in Counseling.
American School Counselor Association. Berks County School Counselor
Association. Certification in PA Student Assistance Program K-12.
Certification in School Culture & Violence. Certification in K-12
Threat Assessment. Chi Sigma Iota Counseling Honor Society
International. Bachelor of Art in Psychology, Millersville University.
Experience in diverse educational settings including CTE, traditional
high schools, IU, partial hospitalization, and behavioral wraparound.
