Dual Enrollment/certification with RACC

Dual enrollment is a partnership between institutions of higher education and school districts allowing high school students to enroll in college courses early and earn transferable college credits.

There are four criteria you have to meet if you want a high-quality dual enrollment program.

Those critera consist of

  • Courses have to match the level of rigor as those taught to traditional college students
  • Instructors match the same expectations as instructors of similar courses and receive appropriate support and evaluation
  • School districts and higher education institutions have to publicly report on student participation
  • Programs undergo evaluation based on the data that is available
